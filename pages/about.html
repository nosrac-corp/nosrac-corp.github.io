Privacy

and our ethics in general

If you think that building an identity platform requires us to have your personal data, well... you'd be right. But trust us, we really don't want it! Your data should be yours, and with that in mind, we collect as little as possible. What we do need to collect, we encrypt and give you the keys so we couldn't go snooping around even if we wanted to (and we really don't want to).

Projects

And how we choose them

We love working on projects that make a meaningful impact on people. We want to make people's lives easier, while also building cool projects that interest us. With that in mind, we try to select projects that make us happy to work on, and that we'd personally want to use. We do of course need to pay the bills so financial viability plays a role in our choices, but we don't want chasing profit to be the guiding star in choosing new projects to work on.
